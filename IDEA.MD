# Marketing Campaign & Asset Management CRM

## Overview
This project is a centralized management application designed for digital marketing teams and product managers. Its core objective is to streamline the lifecycle of launching and testing new products, managing advertising campaigns, and tracking the performance of marketing assets. It serves as a unified hub to replace fragmented spreadsheets and siloed data.

## Core Purpose
Digital marketing involves coordinating multiple elements: identifying products to promote, crafting psychological marketing angles, managing media assets (creatives), and tracking campaign budgets. This system provides a "single source of truth" to organize these workflows, making it easier for teams to analyze performance and make data-driven decisions.

## Key Features & Workflows

### 1. Product & Campaign Pipeline
The application features a Kanban-style pipeline to track products through various testing phases:
- **Planning:** New product ideas queued for market research.
- **Active Campaigns:** Products currently running active advertising tests.
- **Evaluated:** Completed tests with a final verdict (successful or discontinued).
Users can attach documentation, scoring metrics, and next-action notes to each product to keep the team aligned.

### 2. Marketing Asset Library (Angles, Creatives, Hooks)
A successful marketing campaign relies on structured A/B testing. The system organizes this by:
- **Marketing Angles:** Defining the psychological approach for a campaign (e.g., highlighting a "Problem/Solution", showcasing a "Benefit", or creating an "Irresistible Offer"). The system tracks the success rate of each angle.
- **Creatives Management:** Storing and linking media assets (video/image files) to specific campaigns and angles, allowing teams to easily identify top-performing "winner" creatives.
- **Ad Hooks:** A centralized library of attention-grabbing hooks (the first 3 seconds of a video or headline) categorized by platform (e.g., Social Media, Search) and psychological trigger (curiosity, pain points, demonstrations).

### 3. Budget & ROI Tracking
Tracking financial performance is critical to evaluating campaign success:
- **Expense Logging:** The system logs marketing expenditures, such as advertising spend and production costs, allocating them to specific product campaigns.
- **Revenue Tracking:** Revenue generated from specific campaigns is recorded, allowing for automatic Return on Investment (ROI) calculations.
- **Performance Analytics:** By comparing allocated budgets against generated revenue, the platform helps teams quickly identify profitable campaigns and optimize their marketing spend.

## Target Audience
- Digital Marketing Agencies.
- Product Management Teams.
- E-commerce Managers needing to track advertising assets and campaign ROI in one place.

## Summary
In essence, this is a specialized CRM tailored for performance marketing. It answers critical business questions: "Which product are we launching next?", "What marketing angle yields the highest conversion rate?", and "What is the ROI of our current advertising spend?" within a single, streamlined dashboard.
